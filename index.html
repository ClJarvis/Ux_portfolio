Medicine Minder

A Case Study

I worked on a application to notify users when that their prescriptions were running low, so they could reorder before they ran out.

Interviews: Example Questions with Aggregated answers

I interviewed potential users to see if there was a need for my product idea. These were done in person or via email. I complied the response to get a better feel for what my product needed.

    Survey Question
  1. Have you ever ran or almost ran out of a prescription?
    • Yes, happens often,
  2. How do You fell if you run out?
    • Annoyed, Upset, Stupid, Worried, Irresponsible
  3. What would help?
    • Text, Push Notification, email
